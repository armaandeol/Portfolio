Oculoo App: A Seamless Fusion of Hardware and Software

Oculoo is an innovative project that combines hardware and software to create a unique solution for reading and processing medication information. The app works in tandem with a hardware device—a smart spec equipped with a camera—that captures videos or photos of medication labels. These visuals are uploaded to a server where an Optical Character Recognition (OCR) model processes the data to extract critical information. The processed data is then stored securely in Firestore and displayed on the Flutter-built app, ensuring a smooth user experience.

How Oculoo is Helpful

  • Medication Tracking:
    Oculoo simplifies the process of reading and organizing medication details, ensuring users have easy access to accurate and readable information about their prescriptions.
  • Improved Accessibility:
    By converting text from labels into a digital format, the app assists users who may have difficulty reading small print or complex medical jargon.
  • Efficient Data Management:
    The app’s integration with a server and database ensures seamless data retrieval and storage, making it a reliable tool for both patients and healthcare supervisors.

A Collaborative Team Effort

Oculoo stands out as a large-scale project that required teamwork across multiple disciplines. This experience allowed me to engage in various team activities, such as:

  • Cross-functional Collaboration: Coordinating between hardware and software teams to ensure smooth integration.
  • Problem Solving: Overcoming challenges in hardware-software synchronization, OCR accuracy, and data handling.
  • Project Management: Balancing technical and organizational aspects to deliver a cohesive product.

A Blend of Hardware and Software

The project showcases the seamless integration of cutting-edge hardware (smart specs with a camera) and advanced software technologies (Flutter for the app and Python for the OCR model). This synergy makes Oculoo a robust solution for real-world healthcare challenges.
